Quick guide before you start
Tezkor yo‘riqnoma

This speaking test has questions from the server. For each question there is a Preparation time (5 seconds), then Speaking time (30 seconds). Questions unlock one by one.

Timing

  • Preparation: 5 seconds
  • Speaking: 30 seconds
  • Auto-progress: Next question starts automatically

How it works

  • The question is read aloud first.
  • Timer shows: preparation then speaking.
  • REC visual shows during speaking.
  • Answers are automatically recorded and submitted at the end.

Ushbu speaking testi serverdan olingan savollardan iborat. Har bir savol uchun avval Tayyorlov vaqti (5 sekund), so‘ng Gapirish vaqti (30 sekund) beriladi. Savollar ketma-ket ochiladi.

Vaqtlar

  • Tayyorlov: 5 sekund
  • Gapirish: 30 sekund
  • Avtomatik davom: Keyingi savol avtomatik boshlanadi

Qanday ishlaydi

  • Savol avval o‘qib eshittiriladi.
  • Taymer ko‘rinadi: avval Tayyorlov, keyin Gapirish.
  • Gapirayotganda REC indikatori ko‘rinadi.
  • Javoblar avtomatik yozib olinadi va test oxirida yuboriladi.
